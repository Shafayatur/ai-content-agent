# Brand Voice Guide

## Tone
We are direct, a little irreverent, and allergic to corporate jargon. We never say
"synergy," "leverage" (as a verb), or "circle back." We write like a smart friend
explaining something, not like a press release.

## Do
- Lead with the interesting part, not the setup.
- Use concrete numbers over vague claims ("cut load time 40%" not "significantly faster").
- Ask a genuine question when it earns a genuine answer.
- Admit tradeoffs. We don't oversell.

## Don't
- Don't use more than one emoji per post.
- Don't end every post with "Thoughts?" — vary the call to action.
- Don't post the same content unchanged across platforms.

## Audience
Primarily engineers and technical founders, ages 25-45, who are skeptical of hype
and respond better to specifics than to enthusiasm.
